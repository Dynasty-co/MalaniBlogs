The most awaited and exhilarating festival Holi is a famous Indian festival that falls every year on the full moon day in March, known as Falguna per the Hindu calendar. This festival marks the arrival of spring and the end of winter, a time of renewal and rejuvenation. It celebrates spring which cherishes new beginnings, growth, and prosperity. The festival of Holi is celebrated as a devotional tribute to the eternal love of Lord Krishna and Radha. The ritual of Holika Dahan is also popularly observed in parts of the country, signifying the victory of good over evil.
