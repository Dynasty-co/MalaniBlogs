  1. 18 KARAT - This type of gold is made out of 18 parts of gold and 6 parts of other metals like copper or silver etc. 18k gold is mostly desired for making regular-wear jewelry, diamond rings & watches. Also, it is less expensive as compared to 22k & 24k gold. Both 18k white gold and rose gold are more popular in the jewelry industry as 18k gold can hold diamonds firmly.   2. 22 KARAT - This type of gold is comprised of 22 parts of gold and two parts of other metals/alloys like copper, silver, nickel & zinc.
