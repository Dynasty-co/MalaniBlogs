The groom ties the knot of Mangalsutra around the bride's neck is believed to be a prevalent ritual in Hindu Weddings. From traditional single yellow thread/THAALI design to modern black beads embedded with a pendant design, the necklace Mangalsutra has varied from region to region. Generally, black, red, gold and white beads are used to make Mangalsutra. It is commonly believed that the sacred thread of Mangalsutra defines the bonding of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati.
